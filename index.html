🎥 eLearning • Original Framework Design • AI-Enhanced Performance Support

The L.E.A.P. Framework:
Built from Analysis. Designed for Performance.

Phase 1 — Discovery & Analysis
The client had no existing de-escalation framework. I conducted a comprehensive training analysis — including stakeholder analysis, gap analysis, DIF analysis, and training needs analysis — to understand why frontline teams struggled in irate customer situations. Findings revealed the core gap: employees lacked a shared mental model and in-the-moment language they could recall under emotional pressure.
Phase 2 — Framework Creation
Based on the analysis, I created the L.E.A.P. framework from scratch to fit the client’s specific business needs: Listen → Empathize → Apologize → Perform. The acronym was intentionally designed as a cognitive anchor — memorable under pressure, role-ready on day one. I then produced an initial pilot job aid to introduce the framework and validate it with stakeholders.
Phase 3 — AI-Enhanced Redesign + eLearning Course
After piloting the job aid, I identified visual and instructional gaps — cognitive overload, weak hierarchy, no enterprise aesthetic. I used ChatGPT AI workflows to redesign it into an enterprise-ready performance support tool while maintaining full instructional strategy ownership. I also built the full Articulate Storyline eLearning course embedding the framework in scenario-based practice with real coaching scripts.
